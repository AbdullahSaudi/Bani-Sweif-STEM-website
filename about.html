About us!

Applied Engineering Journal Bani Sweif STEM

Website AEJBS.Org; the website is collecting all most majors of engineering nearly 30 majors that give a chance to students in high schools to determine majors what need to study at university, in addition to projects of engineering to benefit the students of STEM schools, Students in college for graduation Projects, the students who need the idea for projects or enter the competitions.
