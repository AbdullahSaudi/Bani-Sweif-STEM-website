Applied Engineering Journal Competition

"Engineering is a design of the world"

Ready to take to the skies?

Applied engineering journal Bani Sweif STEM competition is a unique, worldwide research and design challenge for pre-college youth. The completion provides participants with real-world engineering experience.

Participants in the competition build more than just engineering skills, in addition to learning about coding, teamwork, communication, and entrepreneurship.

The competition has been designed to foster a deeper understanding of STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) besides related concepts, recognize outstanding talent, and prepare the next generation of globally competitive innovators. The competition has been provided entirely online. You can try yourself in the engineering track.
