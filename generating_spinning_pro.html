Generating from spinning

Our project is based generally on producing electricity by pressure in a way to save energy pure kinetic depending machine that derives its power of generating electricity from the force of pressure. The rotational force that the mop causes is used to rotate the gears which are connected to the turbines to generate electricity.

We brought the mop and designed five woody gears (one big gear and four small gears). We used MDF wood which has very low friction and is lighter than white wood, which was used before. After that, We fixed the big gear around the bottom part of the mop that rotates very fast when applying pressure on it; then we connected a generator that generates electricity from the enormous amount of kinetic energy coming from the high number of oscillations of each small gear.
